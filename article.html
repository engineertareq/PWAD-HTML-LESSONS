Understanding the Article Tag in HTML5

The <article> tag in HTML5 is used to represent a self-contained composition in a document, page, application, or site. This could be a blog post, a news story, a forum post, or any other independent piece of content.

Using the <article> tag helps improve the semantic structure of your HTML documents, making it easier for search engines and assistive technologies to understand the content.
